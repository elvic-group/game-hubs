TIC-TAC-TOE

How to Play

Take turns placing your mark (X or O) on the 3x3 grid. The first player to get three in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins. If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, it's a draw.

Game Modes

  • vs AI — Play against the computer. You are X, AI is O.
  • 2 Players — Play locally with a friend. Take turns on the same screen.

AI Difficulty

  • Easy — AI makes random moves
  • Normal — AI blocks your wins and takes obvious opportunities, but sometimes makes mistakes
  • Hard — AI plays optimally using minimax (unbeatable)

Controls

  • Click or tap any empty cell to place your mark
  • Scores are tracked across rounds within a session

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Win/loss stats are stored only in session memory and reset when you close the page. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.
