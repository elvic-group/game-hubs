MINESWEEPER

How to Play

Reveal all cells that do not contain mines. Numbers indicate how many adjacent cells contain mines. Use logic to deduce which cells are safe and which hide mines. Flag cells you suspect contain mines.

Controls

  • Left Click — Reveal a cell
  • Right Click — Place or remove a flag
  • Long Press — Place a flag (mobile)
  • Esc — Return to Menu

Difficulty

  • Easy — 9 x 9 grid, 10 mines
  • Normal — 16 x 16 grid, 40 mines
  • Hard — 16 x 30 grid, 99 mines

Tips

  • Your first click is always safe
  • The mine counter shows total mines minus placed flags
  • Click the smiley face to restart the current game
  • Empty cells with no adjacent mines auto-reveal their neighbors

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your best times are stored locally on your device using localStorage and never leave your browser. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.

Data Storage

  • Best Times — Saved in localStorage under the key minesweeperBest
  • To reset your best times, clear your browser's localStorage for this page
  • No other data is stored
