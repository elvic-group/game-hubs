PING PONG

How to Play

Move your paddle to hit the ball past your opponent. Each time the ball passes a paddle, the other player scores a point. First to reach the winning score wins the match.

Controls

  • Player 1 (Left): W up, S down
  • Player 2 (Right): Arrow Up, Arrow Down
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Esc — Return to Menu
  • On mobile: touch the left/right half of the screen to move that paddle

Game Modes

  • vs AI — Control the left paddle against the computer
  • 2 Players — Play locally with a friend

AI Difficulty

  • Easy — Slow AI reaction, makes mistakes
  • Normal — Balanced speed and accuracy
  • Hard — Fast, precise, and hard to beat

Ball Physics

The ball speeds up slightly with each hit. Where the ball hits the paddle affects its angle — hitting with the edge creates a steeper bounce. The ball resets to center after each point.

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.
