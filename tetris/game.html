TETRIS

How to Play

Stack falling tetrominoes to complete horizontal lines. Completed lines are cleared and award points. The game ends when pieces stack to the top. Speed increases with each level — every 10 lines cleared advances one level.

Controls

  • Arrow Left or A — Move left
  • Arrow Right or D — Move right
  • Arrow Up or W — Rotate
  • Arrow Down or S — Soft drop
  • Space — Hard drop
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Esc — Return to Menu
  • On mobile: use the on-screen buttons

Scoring

  • 1 Line — 100 x Level
  • 2 Lines — 300 x Level
  • 3 Lines — 500 x Level
  • 4 Lines (Tetris) — 800 x Level
  • Soft Drop — 1 point per cell
  • Hard Drop — 2 points per cell

Difficulty

  • Easy — Slower starting speed, gentle acceleration
  • Normal — Balanced speed and acceleration
  • Hard — Fast starting speed, aggressive acceleration

Features

  • Ghost Piece — Shows where your piece will land
  • Next Piece — Preview of the upcoming piece
  • 7 Tetrominoes — I, O, T, S, Z, J, L shapes

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your high score is stored locally on your device using localStorage and never leaves your browser. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.

Data Storage

  • High Score — Saved in localStorage under the key tetrisHighScore
  • To reset your high score, clear your browser's localStorage for this page
  • No other data is stored
