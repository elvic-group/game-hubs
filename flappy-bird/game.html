FLAPPY BIRD

How to Play

Press Space, click, or tap to make the bird flap upward. Navigate through the gaps between pipes without hitting them or the ground. The game gradually speeds up as your score increases.

Controls

  • Space or Click or Tap — Flap
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Esc — Return to Menu

Difficulty

  • Easy — Wider pipe gaps, slower speed
  • Normal — Balanced gaps and speed
  • Hard — Narrow gaps, faster speed

Scoring

You earn 1 point each time you successfully pass through a pair of pipes. Try to beat your high score!

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your high score is stored locally on your device using localStorage and never leaves your browser. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.

Data Storage

  • High Score — Saved in localStorage under the key flappyBirdHighScore
  • To reset your high score, clear your browser's localStorage for this page
  • No other data is stored
