2048

How to Play

Use arrow keys or swipe to slide all tiles in a direction. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one tile with double the value. Reach the 2048 tile to win! You can keep playing after winning to go for a higher score.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD — Slide tiles
  • Z — Undo last move
  • Esc — Return to Menu
  • On mobile: swipe in any direction

Tile Values

  • Each move slides all tiles and spawns a new 2 (90%) or 4 (10%)
  • Matching tiles merge — their values add together
  • A single tile can only merge once per move
  • Plan ahead to avoid filling the board!

Scoring

Your score increases by the value of each merged tile. For example, merging two 16-tiles gives +32 points. Your best score is saved automatically.

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your best score is stored locally on your device using localStorage and never leaves your browser. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.

Data Storage

  • Best Score — Saved in localStorage under the key 2048BestScore
  • To reset your best score, clear your browser's localStorage for this page
  • No other data is stored
