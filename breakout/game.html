BREAKOUT

How to Play

Move the paddle to bounce the ball and destroy all the bricks. Clear all bricks to advance to the next level. You start with 3 lives — lose a life each time the ball falls below the paddle. Ball speed increases with each level.

Controls

  • Mouse or Touch — Move paddle
  • Space or Click — Launch ball
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Esc — Return to Menu

Brick Types

  • Red — 1 hit, 10 points
  • Orange — 1 hit, 20 points
  • Yellow — 1 hit, 30 points
  • Green — 1 hit, 40 points
  • Blue — 2 hits, 50 points
  • Purple — 3 hits, 80 points
  • Silver — 4 hits, 100 points
  • Gold — Indestructible

Power-Ups

Power-ups drop randomly when bricks are destroyed:

  • WIDE — Widens the paddle for 10 seconds
  • MULTI — Splits ball into 3 balls
  • LIFE — Grants an extra life

Difficulty

  • Easy — Slower ball, wider paddle, 5 lives
  • Normal — Balanced speed and paddle, 3 lives
  • Hard — Fast ball, narrow paddle, 2 lives

Privacy

This game runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party. Your high score is stored locally on your device using localStorage and never leaves your browser. No cookies, analytics, tracking, or network requests are used.

Data Storage

  • High Score — Saved in localStorage under the key breakoutHighScore
  • To reset your high score, clear your browser's localStorage for this page
  • No other data is stored
